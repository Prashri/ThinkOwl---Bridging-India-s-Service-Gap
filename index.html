The Problem Landscape: Common Challenges Across Industries

Indian service industries are burdened by shared operational inefficiencies and customer pain points.

Key Operational Pain Points

  • High Volume Inquiries: Industries like telecom, retail, and financial services consistently register the highest volume of consumer complaints, straining resources.
  • Legacy Systems: Outdated, siloed systems (e.g., Fino Payments Bank) hinder real-time data access, integration, and scalability, causing frequent outages.
  • Complex Manual Processes: Intricate workflows with multiple approvals and manual steps (e.g., 5-day telecom order review) are prone to errors and delays.
  • Security & Compliance Burden: Stringent regulations (RBI, DPDPA, IRDAI) demand robust data protection, which legacy systems often fail to provide, leading to penalties.
